Rage levels

0 - Simmering. Noticable to all cīvis de rēgia vītis. Not contagious.
1 - Anger. Same as 0, but contagious. Grants 2 additional magery.
2 - Enraged. Same as 1, highly contagious. Grants 4 additional magery. Danger of insanity.
3 - Wrath. Same as 2, extremely contagious. Grant 6+ additional magery. Blighted soul, insanity.
